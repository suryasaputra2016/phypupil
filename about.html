About us

PhyPupil

PhyPupil is a physics tutoring and learning service. We aim to teach physics to everyone who are curious and interested in learning about the universe.

There are two kind of students: those who hates and those who love physics. We are here for the later. If you think you don't get enough physics at school. And you want to explore more about this subject. We can provide you with materials, one on one tutoring, physics fun activities, intensive physics camp, and much more.

Come and see the wonder of physics together with us!
